Privacy Policy

Effective date: 24.04.2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the app Household Finance ("the App") handles user data.

1. Data Collection and Usage

The App is designed to function primarily offline. All data entered by the user (such as financial records, categories, and calculations) is stored locally on the user's device.

The App does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to external servers.

2. Local Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using internal storage mechanisms. This includes financial data, settings, and user-defined content.

The developer has no access to this data.

3. Data Export and Import

The App may allow users to export and import data (for example via CSV files). These files are created and managed entirely by the user.

The App does not transmit exported data externally.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party services that collect personal data.

If this changes in the future (e.g., analytics or cloud sync), this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you are responsible for securing your device and any exported files.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. No personal data is knowingly collected.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
