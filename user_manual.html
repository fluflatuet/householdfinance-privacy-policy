1. Introduction

Welcome to the Household Finance App! This tool helps households manage shared finances transparently. Whether splitting rent 50/50 or using complex relative splits, we handle the math so you can focus on your goals.

Navigate quickly between all major features using the side drawer.

2. Getting Started

The Household Overview is your central hub for seeing the "big picture". It shows a summary of incomes, expenses, and most importantly, the calculated contribution for each member based on your selected strategy.

3. Managing Members

To add a new member, tap the + button on the Household screen. Members are the foundation of all calculations.

Adding a new member with a custom name and color.

Regular Members: Typically the main earners whose income is used to calculate split ratios.
Special Members: Used for dependents or shared funds (like a 'Joint Pot'). Their income (allowances, child support) is deducted from total expenses before the split is calculated.

6. Managing Goals & Budgets

The Goals section is a powerful tool for both saving up for dreams and limiting spending via budgets.

Main goals screen showing progress bars and recent transactions.

Creating a Savings Goal

When creating a savings goal, you can set a target amount and even include expected interest. This helps you stay motivated as you see the progress bar fill up.

Setting up a new savings goal with a specific target.

Creating a Budget

Budgets are "inverted" goals. Instead of saving up to a target, you set a limit for a specific category or period. The app warns you as you approach your limit.

Defining a maximum spending limit for a budget.

Managing Progress

Add transactions manually or set up Recurring Transactions for regular savings contributions or monthly budget resets.

Automate your finances with recurring goal transactions.
Log interest payments to see your savings grow faster.

Milestones & Trends

Celebrate your progress! The app provides feedback when you reach milestones like being "Halfway Through" a budget or "Almost There" with your savings.

Use the Trends view to see forecasted completion dates for savings or remaining days for a budget based on your transaction history.

7. Contribution Models

Choose the strategy that fits your household's values. You can change this at any time in the settings.

ModelDescription
50/50 SplitEqual share for everyone, regardless of income.
Relative SplitProportional to each member's share of total income.
Fixed AmountEach member keeps a fixed sum; the rest goes into the shared pot.
Joint AccountAll income goes into one pot; all expenses come out.
Switch between contribution strategies with a single tap.

8. Settings & Security

Your privacy is paramount. All data is encrypted using SQLCipher and stored only on your device. We never see your financial data.

  • Biometrics: Enable Fingerprint or Face Unlock for extra security.
  • Data Management: Export to CSV for external analysis or create encrypted backups.
  • Localization: Customize your currency symbol and app language.
↑ Back to Menu

9. Best Practices

Pro Tip: Use the "Shared Member" for expenses that benefit the whole household, like groceries or utilities.
  • Frequency Matters: Ensure all incomes and expenses are set to the same frequency (e.g., Monthly) for the most accurate "Household Overview" comparison.
  • Review Trends: Check the Trends section once a month to see if your spending habits are shifting.
  • Backup Often: Since data is local, a device loss means data loss. Use the Encrypted Backup feature regularly!
